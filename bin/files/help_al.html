Working with buttons.

You can use the buttons on the left side to add patient by pressing the "Add Patient" buttton,then you can fill the fields.

You can see the "add" button press it and the pacient is added.

After the patient is added you can see it by pressing the button "Show Pacients".

Now you can see a table with the infos for the pacients that exist.

Click in the pacient that you added and then press the button "Add Visit",and fill the fields that will be showned,and at the end click the button "Add".

After that for the selected pacient you can se its all visits by clicking the button "Show Visits".

You can update the infos for the patient or the visit by selecting visit or patient record and then clicking on the button "Update" or "Delete"

Be careful after deleting a patient you delete its all visits.

When clicking on the button "Print Guidilance" you can print a medical guidilance for the pacient that is selected.

Working with menu bar.

On the menu bar at the file opsion you can exit the program or restart the program.

On the menu bar at the edit opsion you can change the theme of the program .

On the menu bar at the account opsion you can log out out of the program .

Working with status bar.

On the status bar you can do basically all the things you can do with the buttons on the left side of the program.

So you can add,update and delete patient ,print a medical guidilance. .
